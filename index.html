COFFEE

What is Coffee?

A cup of coffee is a hot drink made from the roasted and ground seeds of a tropical shrub. Although coffee is typically viewed as a dark brown color, the seeds themselves can be red, yellow, and orange. Coffee is often a part of people's daily routines, especially those who are setting goals or needing to meet a deadline. In America, at least one out of three adults drink one or two cups of coffee when they wake up. 😀

Flavors of Coffee

Some of the most popular flavors of coffee include french vanilla, hazelnut, peppermint, pumpkin, caramel, and mocha. Most coffee drinkers consider these to be their favorite choice and they are typically available year-round. ♥♥♥♥♥
